NXT Museum

About us and our history

Nxt Museum, the young entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen wanted to exhibit large-scale art installations, which are developed from interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians and scientists. On August 29, 2020, the museum opened their doors for the first time in a production studio in the heart of Amsterdam. Amsterdam is ideal for discovering, creating and exhibiting media art, so it's perfect for a museum like this. This form of art experimented with technological possibilities and applications; dynamic and not bound to a form. This space would offer a breeding ground for this form of art; a place where different art movements and sciences come together and can be exhibited.

On display

  • Living Room

    The Living Room installation delves into the concept of space as a living entity: a continuously shifting architectural world that has its own powers of perception.

  • Presence and Erasure

    The digital artwork Presence and Erasure by Random International creates temporary portraits of passers-by. Without the participation of the passing human bodies, the canvas is blank.

  • Dimensional Sampling

    The work explores the global rise of QR codes, such as in China where the codes are fully integrated into everyday life. From purchasing products to identifying seniors.

  • Life in our Minds: Motherflock III

    With Motherflock, Random International presents a virtual flock of origami-like "birdoid" objects.

  • Swarm Study XIII
